2. Information We Collect

This website is designed to collect as little data as possible.

  • Data You Provide Directly: The product name you enter and the answers you provide to the AI-generated questions.
  • Data We Do Not Collect: We do not collect or store any personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email address, or IP address in our database.

3. How We Use Your Information

The data you provide is used for the core functionality of the website:

  • To generate contextual questions via an external AI service.
  • To generate the final assessment and score via the AI service.
  • To display the product name and score in the "Recent Assessments" list.

4. Data Sharing with Third Parties (External AI Provider)

To generate the assessments, we use the services of Google (for the Gemini/Google AI API). The product name, questions, and your answers are sent to Google's servers for processing. We encourage you to review Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy.

5. Data Storage

We store the submitted product name and the calculated score in a database provided by Netlify (Netlify Database) for the sole purpose of public display in the "Recent Assessments" list.

Important Notice: As the submitted product names may be publicly visible, we urge you not to enter any personal, sensitive, or confidential information into the product name input field.

6. Cookies

This website does not use cookies for user tracking or analytics. Only technically necessary cookies may be set by our hosting providers for basic site functionality.
